Meet Our Team

Philip Heinz

CEO & Founder

Philip Heinz is the person who started Hot Beans Web. He began the company all by himself back in 2011. Over time, he made it grow into what it is now. Philip is the big boss of the whole company. He owns most of the company too. Without Philip, there would be no Hot Beans Web.

Dave Cherry

Chief Operations Officer

Dave Cherry is a very important person at Hot Beans. He's the boss of the web development team and oversees all the work done in the office. Dave has been working at Hot Beans since 2011. Even before that, he worked with Philip Heinz since 2005. Together, they both played a big part in making Hot Beans Web the successful company it is now.

Matt James

Head of Marketing

Matt James is a web developer at Hot Beans Web. Right now, he's busy with many projects for different clients. He's even leading two big projects for the company. Matt is super important to our team at Hot Beans. Without awesome developers like him, we couldn't make such great stuff.
