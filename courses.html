Courses

At Hot Beans Web, we have different courses to help you understand how websites work and how we create them for customers. There are three levels of these courses:

BEGINNER

These classes are for people who are new to web development and want to learn the basics. We're sharing some websites we enjoy and believe you'll like them too.

kodilla codecademy

INTERMIDATE

These classes are for people who know a little about web development and want to get better at it by learning new and harder techniques. We're sharing some websites we enjoy and think you'll like them too.

ADVANCED

These classes are for people who know a lot about web development and want to get even better by learning advanced skills and techniques. We're sharing some websites we enjoy and think you'll like them too.
